Generation NOW has been selected as a 2022-2024 United Way Systems Change Partner!

We are one of 86 partner organizations selected for an investment-based focus from the United Way. This partnership is a two-year commitment between Generation NOW and the United Way of Greater Cincinnati. We will work together to plan, establish goals, and accelerate systems change in our region.

Key areas of focus will include:

• Equitable Economic Mobility: Breaking down barriers to employment & equitable asset building

• Housing First: Building strong financial foundations through stable housing

• Improved Prevention, Stronger Communities: Scaling trauma-informed approaches to strengthen community well-being

• NextGen Success: Financial empowerment for young people through coordinated educational and workforce pathways

• Ready Kids, Resilient Families: Transforming the education system of care to support the resiliency of children and their families

• Responding to Community Need: Improving the effectiveness of the nonprofit system of care to respond to community needs

Did you know that you can link your Kroger Shopper’s Card to automatically earn rewards for Generation NOW?

It’s simple! Just follow these steps:

1. Go to http://www.kroger.com and log in

2. Go to the Community Rewards page

3. Search for Generation NOW Cincinnati Network either by name or code SV139

4. Click Enroll

5. Shop as you normally do!

Every time you swipe your Kroger Shopper’s Card at checkout, Kroger will make a donation to Generation NOW at no cost to you. It’s the easy way to show your support!

*New users will need to create an account, which requires some basic information, a valid email address, and a rewards card.

Do you know an amazing young professional in our area?

Many young professionals in Cincinnati find it difficult to develop their personal and professional brands when they don’t get recognized for what they’re doing. They often feel that the best way to grow as a professional is to move to other cities where that type of recognition and advancement are available.

We want to show Cincinnati’s young professionals that they can grow and thrive here. Generation NOW is providing a platform to honor their accomplishments, which in turn leads to more achievements, successes, and growth.

Do you know a young professional who deserves to be recognized for everything they’re doing? Submit your nomination now!
